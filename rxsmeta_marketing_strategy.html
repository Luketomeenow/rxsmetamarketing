Content System
Content pillars and production cadence
RXS Meta needs a content system that generates authority, trust, and pipeline — consistently. Here are the 5 pillars and a weekly rhythm.
Pillar 1 — AI in Philippine Business
Weekly insight on how AI is reshaping hospitality, education, logistics, or government in the PH context. Formats: LinkedIn text post, short-form video, carousel. Angle: practical, local, actionable — not generic AI hype.
Pillar 2 — ESG Made Measurable
Demystify ESG for Philippine businesses. What SEC mandates. How to start reporting. Tools and frameworks. Positions RXS Meta as the go-to guide for ESG compliance tech — a blue ocean in the PH market.
Pillar 3 — Client Proof & Case Studies
Document real transformations: before/after metrics, ROI achieved, challenges solved. Even anonymized mini-cases build trust. One per month, repurposed across all channels.
Pillar 4 — Yen's Advocacy & Leadership
Responsible riding, education, health, and community stories. This humanizes the brand and builds mass appeal beyond the enterprise buyer. Keeps media relationships warm.
Pillar 5 — Tech Deep Dives
Explainers on AR/VR, Blockchain, Metaverse, AI loyalty programs. Establishes thought leadership with the tech-savvy segment and drives inbound interest from innovation-minded buyers.
Weekly production rhythm
Mon: LinkedIn thought piece (Pillar 1 or 2) — Tue: Short-form video/Reel — Wed: Partner or advocacy spotlight — Thu: LinkedIn engagement (reply, comment, reshare) — Fri: Newsletter digest + 1 case study per month

Lead Generation System
Inbound + outbound pipeline architecture
A dual-track system: inbound authority content pulls warm leads, outbound ABM hunts cold prospects. Both tracks feed into a CRM-driven nurture sequence.
Inbound track
1
LinkedIn content → profile visits → DM inquiry
Optimize Yen's LinkedIn profile with a strong CTA ("Schedule a 30-min strategy call"). Every post drives profile traffic. Target: 10 qualified DMs per week from content alone.
2
Lead magnet → email nurture → consultation booking
The AI readiness assessment or ESG checklist captures emails. Automate a 5-email sequence: deliver value, share case studies, overcome objections, and CTA to book a call. Use ActiveCampaign or MailerLite.
3
Media appearances → website traffic → CTA conversion
Every radio/TV appearance drives search spikes. Ensure rxsmeta.com has fast load time, a clear "Let's Talk" form, and a Calendly embed. Capture the spike.
Outbound track (Clay-powered ABM)
1
Build target account list in Clay
250 accounts across 5 verticals: Hotels (Cebu/BGC), Private schools (Metro Manila), Hospitals (private networks), Facilities companies, Listed PH conglomerates. Enrich with decision-maker contacts, LinkedIn URLs, and recent news.
2
Personalized 3-touch email sequence
Touch 1: "Saw you're expanding [X property] — here's how [competitor type] cut ops cost by 30% with facilities AI." Touch 2: ESG compliance angle. Touch 3: Direct ask for 20-min call. Personalize using Clay enrichment data.
3
LinkedIn connection + warm message sequence
Parallel to email: connect with decision-makers on LinkedIn with a personalized note. Send a value message (not a pitch) after connection. Reference shared interests or advocacy if visible on their profile.
4
CRM pipeline management (HubSpot or Pipedrive)
All leads — inbound and outbound — flow into a CRM. Stages: Identified → Contacted → Interested → Proposal → Closed. Weekly pipeline review. Automate follow-up reminders and drip sequences per stage.
AI Agents & Automation Stack
Best tools for RXS Meta — by function
RXS Meta is an AI consultancy — its own internal stack should be a living proof-of-concept for clients. Here's the recommended automation architecture.
Tier 1 — Core sales & marketing automation
Clay
Already connected
Lead enrichment, ABM list-building, AI-powered personalization at scale. Use for: building target account lists across hotels/schools/hospitals, enriching with decision-maker emails and LinkedIn, and triggering personalized outreach sequences. This is your outbound engine.
HubSpot or Pipedrive CRM
Essential
Central pipeline management. Automate lead routing, follow-up reminders, email sequences, and deal stage tracking. HubSpot's free tier is strong; Pipedrive is leaner for a small team. Integrate with Clay for seamless lead flow.
Apollo.io or Instantly.ai
Outbound email
Automated cold email sequences at scale with inbox rotation, deliverability optimization, and reply tracking. Use Instantly for high-volume cold outreach (50–100 emails/day). Pair with Clay-enriched lists for maximum personalization.
Tier 2 — Content & social media automation
Buffer or Taplio
Content scheduling
Schedule LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube posts in advance. Taplio is specifically built for LinkedIn authority building — AI-assisted post writing, scheduling, and analytics. Recommended for Yen's personal brand growth.
Claude (Anthropic) as content co-pilot
AI writing
Use Claude to generate first drafts of LinkedIn posts, case study writeups, newsletter editions, email sequences, and website copy — all trained on RXS Meta's brand voice. Cuts content production time by 70%.
Beehiiv or Substack
Newsletter
Launch "The RXS Intel" newsletter. Beehiiv has strong growth tools and referral programs. Automate welcome sequences, segment subscribers by interest (ESG vs. AI tech), and track opens/clicks to identify hot leads.
Tier 3 — Operations & client delivery automation
Make (Integromat) or n8n
Workflow automation
The backbone of RXS Meta's internal automation. Connect CRM → Clay → email → Slack → calendar. Automate: new lead notifications, proposal generation triggers, client onboarding checklists, and reporting workflows. n8n is self-hostable and ideal for an AI-forward consultancy.
Notion + Notion AI
Knowledge & ops
Central knowledge base for RXS Meta: SOPs, client deliverables, project management, and research. Notion AI can auto-summarize meeting notes, generate project briefs, and search across all internal docs — great for a growing consulting team.
Custom AI Agent (LangGraph / Claude API)
Flagship product demo
Build an internal AI agent that handles: inbound inquiry triage, meeting scheduling, proposal drafting from a template, and ESG assessment scoring. This doubles as a live demo for enterprise prospects — "this is what we can build for you."
Gamma or Beautiful.ai
Pitch decks
AI-powered presentation generation. Use for rapid proposal decks and pitch materials tailored to each prospect. Dramatically reduces time-to-proposal for the sales team.
